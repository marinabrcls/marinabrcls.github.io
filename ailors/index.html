Ailors is built daily on a concept that blends the responsible aspects of fashion needed for the new century with the technology that makes every stitch valuable and unique.

Each product has an authenticity tailored into it. Committed to environmental issues alongside the best-sourced fabrics around the globe, flawless details, technology, and precise tailoring Ailors guarantee a unique experience in fashion.

Murilo Tadashi

CREATIVE DIRECTOR

Murilo Tadashi studied information systems at the university but soon realized that this was not the sea he wanted to sail.
He started in fashion in editorial work, then cool hunting and in 2014 started his fashion design graduation at the European Design Institute (IED) and discovered that creating was his passion.
Now, at Ailors, Murilo can blend all this background in technology and fashion, building Ailors creative vision and searching for the best fabrics and suppliers across the world.
